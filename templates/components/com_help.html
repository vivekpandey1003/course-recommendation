Need Help?

We’re here to assist you! Below are some of the most common questions we get. If you still need help, feel free to contact us.

How do I start using EduSelect?
Just click on "Get Started" on the home page and follow the steps to get personalized course recommendations based on your preferences.
Are the course recommendations free?
Yes, our recommendation engine is completely free to use. However, enrolling in the course may require payment on the respective learning platform.
Can I filter courses by difficulty or price?
Absolutely. Use our filters on the course listing page to narrow down courses by difficulty level, duration, price, and more.
What if I can’t find a course on the topic I want?
We constantly update our database. You can contact us directly and we'll try to include more relevant courses in future updates.
